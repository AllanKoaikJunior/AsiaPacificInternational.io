Funding Size

In USA$50,000 and up (typical transactions range from $500,000 to $500 million Outside USA $1 million minimum

Pricing (typical--may vary on a case by case basis)

Most debt financing transactions the interest rate is 4% to 12% per annum. Discount Factoring--1% per month Purchase Order financing--1% per month Real Estate Transaction--3% to 12% annual rate

Time Line to Close

Bridge Loans can be completed within ten days Most other transactions from 30 to 90 days

Fees

There is no upfront processing fee. There usually is a nominal, refundable retainer fee required AFTER the Clearing House has obtained a pre-approval from a lender with specific terms based on information the borrower has provided in a loan worksheet the borrower will be asked to complete and submit. When funding is completed the borrower will pay a success fee (commission) of usually 2% of the loan amount to the Clearing House.
